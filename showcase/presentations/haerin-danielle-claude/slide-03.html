03 · On Stage · The harmony as connective tissue

Their harmony is the group's spine.

Across the singles, Haerin and Danielle keep handing the melody back and forth — sometimes inside a single bar. It's the most quietly engineered part of NewJeans.

TRK · 01 · 2022
